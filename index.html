About Me

I have recently obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics, I am right at the beginning of my scientific career and production with a strong foundation in mathematical analysis. My main interests reside in functional analysis, complex analysis, operator theory and harmonic analysis. I am passionate about teaching and I am experienced in presenting research at international conferences, building strong connections within the global mathematical community. I am currently a Postdoc researcher at Laval University, Québec City (CA). This website was last updated On December 3rd, 2025.
